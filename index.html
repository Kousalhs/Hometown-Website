Top four activities to do in Kozani

Visit a Museum

The Historical, Folklore and Natural History Museum of Kozani is a wonderful destination for anyone who loves history, tradition and nature.

Go for a picnic

Enjoy a picnic, soak up the sun or just relax at the beautiful Deer Park of Agios Panteleimon in Agios Dimitrios, Kozani.

Explore the nature

Skepasmeno Gorge is a stunning natural spot, full of waterfalls, lush forests and wildlife.

Visit a church

Visit the Holy Monastery of Saint John Vazelon in Agios Dimitrios, Kozani. A reborn spiritual landmark where faith, history, and Pontic heritage meet.
